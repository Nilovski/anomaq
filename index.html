How it works

⬡ QAE + qPCA — why they win
Amplitude encoding preserves magnitude information via a padding qubit. Anomaly score is the reconstruction residual — inter-feature correlations in Hilbert space are invisible to classical detectors.
⚠ QKernel + QAOA — known limits
Quantum Kernel is O(N²) — slow on large sets and shows OCSVM saturation at small n. QAOA is capped at ~16 samples (statevector RAM). Both improve with re-uploading layers and larger circuits.
